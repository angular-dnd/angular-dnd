Example of @angular-dnd/tree View Source Codes

The drag-and-drop tree was designed with easy to use in mind.

    <angular-dnd-tree
      [treeId]="treeId"
      [rootItem]="rootItem"
      [spec]="treeSpec"
    >
      <ng-template treeItem let-node>
			  <your-item-card [treeNode]="node"></your-item-card>
      </ng-template>
    </angular-dnd-tree>

Where is a template of your-item-card can look something like:

    <div>
      <b>{{item.title}}</b>
    </div>
